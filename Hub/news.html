TOSHIHIRO NAGOSHI AND DAISUKE SATO
LEAVING RGG STUDIO AND SEGA







In the note celebrating Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio's
10th anniversary, aside from the note from Yokoyama
there were also notes by Nagoshi and Sato
stating that both of them are leaving SEGA and RGG Studio.
However, they have high hopes for the studio, as they taught them all
they knew before their departures.
