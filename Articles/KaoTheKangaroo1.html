KAO THE KANGAROO

The Polish video game market in the late 1990s and early 2000s wasn't that reknown as it is today. The ideal example should be the game titled Kao the Kangaroo, which, even though it released worldwide on PC and Dreamcast, didn't get much attention and success outside of its homeland - Poland.
