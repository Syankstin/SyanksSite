DEVIL MAY CRY

When CAPCOM was working on Resident Evil 4 they came around a bug which, after attacking an enemy, the said enemy was thrown up into the air. CAPCOM thought then that they should make it more action oriented, but then... they thought about making a new franchise entirely because that game was too different from any other Resident Evil title. This is a short version of the events that made Devil May Cry - an action game that is often called "the granddad of all slasher games". In case you don't know what a slasher game is let me tell you this: "Do you know what God of War or Ninja Gaiden are?" If your response is: "yes", then you know what slasher games are. And Devil May Cry is one of those games and preceded them.
