COWBOY BEBOP


Anime is definitely a medium like any other. Like films, anime can be a comedy, slice of life, heart-warming romance, action packed series, or... mafia drama. Yes, Japanese creators often take the topic that was depicted in films like Godfather, or Scarface. Anime that takes the topic of Italian mafia in United States is none other than 91 Days. It's a series about Avilio Bruno, or rather Angelo Lagusa who is the only living member of the Lagusa family. As a young child he saw the moment of his family's deaths by the hands of the Vanetti family. Later in the first episode we see him as a young man who returns to Lawless city and vows that he'll get his revenge on Vanetti family - the mafia family that killed his family.
