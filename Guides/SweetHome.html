Sweet Home (Nintendo Entertainment System / Nintendo Family Computer)
Release Year: 1989 (Japan)
Version: 0.1
Author: Syanks
Contact info: Look at the footer ;)

Northern Europe. September, 1995.
On a rather average September day, Jennifer Simpson and her friends are adopted from the Granite Orphanage by a woman named Mary. She takes them to her new home, a massive mansion, marked by an astonishing, monolithic Clock Tower. All seems to be normal, but nothing feels quite right.

It all happened in September of that exact year...
